# Unused FAQ questions — saved for future use

## What's the minimum engagement length?
Fractional CPO work runs a minimum of three months — strategy needs time to materialize and compound. Advisory retainers run month-to-month. Coaching and workshops can be as short as a single session. The right length becomes clear once we understand what you're solving for.

## How quickly can we get started?
Typically within 1–2 weeks. The first step is a 30-minute call to understand the situation and see if it's a good fit. If it is, we move fast.
